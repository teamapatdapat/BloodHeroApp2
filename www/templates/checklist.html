Who can Donate Blood?

  • -Are in good health
  • -Are between 16-35yrs old(16-17 needs parents consent)
  • -Weight atleast 110 pounds
  • -Have a blood prssure between:Systolic:90-160mmHg, Diastolic:60-100 mmHg:
  • -Pass the physical and health history assestment.

Preparation before Donating Blood

  • 1.Have enough rest and sleep.
  • 2.No alcohol intake 24hrs prior to blood donation.
  • 3.No medication for atleast 24hrs prior to blood donation
  • 4.Have someting to eat prior to blood donation, avoid fatty foods.
  • 5.Drink plenty of fluid, like water or juice.

Steps in Donating Blood

  • 1.Have yout taken.
  • 2.Register and honestly complete the donor registration form.
  • 3.Have your blood type and hemoglobin checked.
  • 4.A physicain wil conduct a blood donor examination..
  • 5.Actual donation-- the amount of blood to be donated(either 350cc or 450cc) will defend on the donors weight and blood pressure it takes usually 10 mins or less.
  • 6.A 5 to 10 mins of rest and plenty of fluid intake are necessary after donation,

What to do After Blood Doantion?

  • 1.Drink plenty of fluid, like water or juice.
  • 2.Refrain from stooping after blod donation.
  • 3.Refrain from strenuous activities like:

    a.Lifting heavy objects

    b.Driving big vehicles such as bus,trucks, and etc.

    c.Operating big machines.

  • 4.Avoid using the punctured arm in lifting heavy objects.
  • 5.Apply pressure on the punctured site and lift the arm incase the site is still bleeding.
  • 6.If there is discoloration and swelling on the punctured site, you may apply cold compress for 24hrs.
  • 7.If there is dizziness, just lay down your feet elevated. Drink plenty of juice, and in just few minutes or so, it will pass.
